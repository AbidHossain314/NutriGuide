Help & FAQ

How is my calorie target calculated?

We use the Mifflin-St Jeor equation, a widely accepted formula, combined with your activity level to estimate your daily energy needs. We then adjust this number based on your health goal (weight loss, gain, or maintenance).

Can I regenerate my meal plan?

Yes! Simply go back to the home page and click the "View Your Plan" button. You'll be taken back to the onboarding form, where you can adjust your preferences and generate a new plan.

What do I get with NutriGuide Pro?

NutriGuide Pro unlocks advanced features like detailed progress analytics, the ability to sync data from wearable devices, and our upcoming photo-based meal logging feature.
